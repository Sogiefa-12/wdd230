Abuja Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

The Purpose

The Abuja Chamber of commerce site is built mainly to promote the business interest of the members of the organization and to promote the area, attracting additional business from nearby states and countries from far and wide.

Scenarios

What events will the chamber be holding this year to promote business-to-business networking

The Abuja Chamber of commerce will be organizing an all inclusive trade fair in the city center to promote and show case the business of all members.

Where can I find the contact information for the chambers board of directors?

To access the contact info of all the board of directors including and past and current serving directors, visit the Board of Directors Info Page in the main website

What has been the population growth in the area?

At present the population growth is 75% increase from the previous years growth
